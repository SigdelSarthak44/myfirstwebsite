    • About Me:

    My name is Sarthak Sigdel. I am 16 years old. I have just compleated my SEE Examinations from Nightingale International Secondary school. I live in Kupondole, Lalitpur. The school that from where i compleated my SEE is located at the heart of Kupondole near the bank of bagmati river. I like to read famous novels and like to write about various aspects that I had came across in my life. My family consists of four. There is me, my brother, my mother and my father. My father is a government employee. He works in Nepal Administrative Staff College. My permanent address is Suklagandaki Munucipality-11, Tanahun, Aalaiche. My village is located at the broder of two districts Syangia and Tanahun. Ity is still lacking the push for developement that it should have gotten looking at the other villages near mine. It is also in a danger zone as of the high danger of cheetah, bear, and tiger.
